## What to do if they reply

- **Positive ("we'd love to embed it")** → send them straight to `openairatlantic.com/embed/stratford` and offer to help with placement/sizing. Don't oversell — you've already won the embed.
- **Curious ("how does this work, what's the catch")** → it's free because OpenAir Atlantic is bootstrapped to be a free public-good service for Atlantic Canada residents. Data comes from Environment Canada (free), DFO (free), Open-Meteo (free). No ads. No data collection beyond standard analytics.
- **"Send this to the CAO"** → DON'T. The CAO position has been vacant since March 2026. Reply with: *"Happy to wait until your CAO situation is settled — let me know who the right contact is when the time is right."*
- **No response after 10 business days** → one polite nudge: *"Just bumping this in case it got buried — totally understand if it's not a fit."* No further follow-up after that.
