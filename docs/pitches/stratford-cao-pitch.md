### What this is

OpenAir Atlantic has built a free, ad-free, AI-powered live-conditions resource for Stratford at **openairatlantic.com/town/stratford**. It pulls real-time data from Environment Canada, Fisheries & Oceans Canada, ECCC air quality, and Open-Meteo, and translates it into plain-English verdicts on whether right now is good for outdoor plans in Stratford.

What residents see when they land:

- A live verdict (Excellent / Good / Fair / Stay Inside) for current conditions
- Beach-by-beach Go/Caution/Skip ratings for Tea Hill, Keppoch, and Kinlock — pulled per-beach with their own coordinates
- Walking verdicts for Cotton Park, Pondside Park, Reddin Park, Fullerton's Creek, Kinlock Park, and Keppoch Park
- Hillsborough Bridge live wind reading (with the Charlottetown commute angle)
- Tea Hill water temperature from the Northumberland Strait DFO buoy
- Tide times, sunrise/sunset, a 7-day forecast, a precipitation radar map centered on town
- A tonight + tomorrow morning planning card and Environment Canada severe-weather alerts when active

The page sends traffic *to* townofstratford.ca rather than competing with it — every park and beach card on the page links to the official town site for hours, amenities, and program info. There are over 40 outbound links to townofstratford.ca on the page today.

OpenAir Atlantic offers a free embeddable widget at **openairatlantic.com/embed/stratford** that surfaces live Stratford conditions on any town webpage in one line of HTML. Three sizes (compact, standard, hero), no subscription, no API key, no tracking. Embedding it on the homepage, recreation pages, or residents portal would add a continuously-updated information service that residents value — without your team writing or maintaining a single line of code.

### Practical details

- **Cost to the Town:** $0. Operating costs are covered by OpenAir Atlantic.
- **Data sources, all free public APIs:** Environment Canada (MSC GeoMet, AQHI, severe weather alerts), Fisheries & Oceans Canada (tide stations), Open-Meteo (forecasts).
- **Data accuracy:** Every metric is sourced from the same federal services your EMO and infrastructure teams already use.
- **Privacy:** No tracking beyond standard web analytics. No resident data collected. No ads. No paywall.
- **Maintenance:** OpenAir Atlantic owns it end-to-end. The Town is never on the hook for uptime, content, or fixes.
